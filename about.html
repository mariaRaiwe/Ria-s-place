About Us.


Ria's place is all about creating beautiful and foodful moments and we are delighted to have you check in on us every moment.
We are foodie inspirational and we promote healthy eating life styles and also provide healthy tips on farm fresh recipes because we know that health is wealth!

This Website is a project work from the code Lagos class 2018 session developed by Raiwe Maria From the web design class. I am truly grateful to the governor Of Lagos state, gov. Akinwunmi Ambode for this vision and for empowering young people Of Lagos state through this platform.
The food images used on this website were not created by me and so I want to acknowledge all those whose images were used to achieve this work. Your creations have been inspirational, Mouth watering and beautiful. Thank you!

I am a 400 level student of the Federal University of technology, owerri studying information management technology. I have always loved to design and create beautiful things. Lately I discovered a new flare for beautifully presented food and how pleasing and convincingly inviting it could be to your senses, and got inspired to create my website based on this new discovery.

This is to all Foodies, Chefs, and everyone out there Who appreciates organic foods. . Ria's place wants you to stay healthy, eat healthy and promote a healthy lifestyle. Feel free to sign up for free tips on healthy farm foods and how to make one yourself, for a healthy you, is a healthy Lagos!
